Our Story

Blending Tradition With Global Culinary Artistry

At Ceylon Fusion, we believe that dining is an adventure of heritage. Sri Lankan cuisine is famous for its bold heat and complex aromatics, but we didn't stop there. We take classic island preparations—like slow-cooked crab curry and fresh claypot dhal—and elevate them with modern textures and presentations from around the world.

Whether you are a local seeking a nostalgic flavor served with a modern flair, or a foreign traveler eager to explore the spices of Sri Lanka without overwhelming fire, our kitchen is designed to be your home.

Meet Executive Chef Nuwan Perera

"Authentic Sri Lankan cooking is an ancestral art. By introducing contemporary culinary methods, we create bridges that let foreign guests discover our flavors while giving locals an exciting new perspective."

With over 15 years heading Michelin-starred fusion dining rooms in London and Melbourne, Chef Nuwan returned to Colombo to redefine contemporary island gastronomy. His signature style focuses on hyper-local ingredients, precise sous-vide techniques, and artistic plating.
